Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is Ilford a good area to invest in property?

Yes, Ilford is one of the most promising areas in East London, with property prices showing steady growth and strong rental demand due to its excellent transport links and local amenities.

2. How much do estate agents in Ilford charge?

Fees vary but typically range from 1% to 3% for sales and 8% to 15% for lettings management. Always check what services are included in the quoted fee.

3. Can I find affordable housing in Ilford?

Ilford offers a range of properties from budget-friendly flats to larger family homes. Compared to central London, it remains relatively affordable.

4. Do I need a solicitor if I use an estate agent?

Yes, even with an estate agent, legal conveyancing must be handled by a solicitor to finalize the purchase or sale of property.

5. What documents do I need to rent a property in Ilford?

Typically, tenants need ID, proof of income, bank statements, and a reference from a previous landlord. Agents will guide you through the exact requirements.
